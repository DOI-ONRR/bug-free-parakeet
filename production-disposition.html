Production Disposition

Monthly production disposition volumes from ONRR's OGOR-B form, covering oil and gas production from federal lands, federal waters, and Native American lands. Data shows how each product was used — sold, injected, transferred, flared, vented, or lost.

Date RangeJan 2016 – Mar 2026
ProductsOil & Gas
Disposition Types35 codes
Last UpdatedJuly 2, 2026
Release FrequencyMonthly
Production disposition data is updated monthly, but final numbers aren't available for 3–4 months. The most recent data will be approximately 3–4 months prior to the current date.
